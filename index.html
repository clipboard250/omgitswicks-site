About

Josh Robinson—better known as OMGItsWicks—is a Fort Myers comedian who built one of Florida's largest social media followings by doing one thing well: telling the truth about what it's actually like to live here.

```

"Is it wet? Then it has gators."

— on the Florida pond question

He grew up in Southwest Florida and has spent years learning the rest of the state one show at a time—Panhandle to Keys, small clubs to sold-out theaters. Gators, Publix density, snowbirds, pythons, tourists encountering things Floridians stopped noticing years ago.

His shows are family-friendly. No shock value, no cheap shots. Every show ends with a free meet and greet—no upcharge, no velvet rope. He's been doing it from the start.
