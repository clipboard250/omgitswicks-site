About

Josh Robinson—better known as OMGItsWicks—is a Fort Myers comedian who built one of Florida's largest social media followings by doing one thing well: telling the truth about what it's actually like to live here.

"Is it wet? Then it has gators."

— on the Florida pond question

His shows are family-friendly. No shock value, no cheap shots. Every show ends with a free meet and greet—no upcharge, no velvet rope.
